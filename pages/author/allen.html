About Allen

Building technology that works for real businesses.

Allen is the Founder and CEO of Ovia Tech LLC, a US-based software development, SaaS, and digital marketing company headquartered in East Meadow, New York. He has over 10 years of professional experience as a full-stack developer, graphic designer, and digital growth strategist, having delivered projects for clients across the United States, Canada, and internationally.

Allen's technical expertise spans the full product development lifecycle: from UI/UX design and front-end development to backend architecture, API integrations, and mobile app development using Flutter. On the digital marketing side, he specializes in SEO strategy, Google Ads management, and conversion-focused web systems that drive measurable revenue growth for US small and mid-sized businesses.

Through Ovia Tech, Allen leads a team delivering fixed-price, fast-turnaround web and SaaS solutions to businesses across industries including healthcare, real estate, construction, e-commerce, and professional services. Ovia Tech's clients consistently see improvements in online visibility, lead generation, and business efficiency after working with the agency.

Allen writes on the Ovia Tech blog to share practical, no-jargon guidance for US business owners who want to understand and use technology as a growth tool, not just a cost. His articles draw directly from real client projects, real results, and over a decade of experience building things that work.
